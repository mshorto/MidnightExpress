How it works

"It's like pooling money with your friends to rent a bus to drive you home. Except, instead of friends it's random people and everything is organized for you. All you have to do is donate and show up." - Midnight Express

Optimized Routes

We gather data to create bus routes that go where people want to go, when they want to go there. Tell us what times and stops you want added to help us build our next route.

Donate to Ride

A donation of $15 toward the bus you want to ride saves you a seat on that bus. We use the crowdfunding site tilt to fund buses. If the minimum tilt amount is not hit by 12 hours prior to a scheduled departure, no supporter will be charged for their donation and that bus will not run.

Spread the Word

You can help a bus reach it's tilt goal by spreading the word. Tell your friends and share on your favorite social media channels to make sure that bus runs.

Climb Aboard

Once a bus hits it's donation goal, we will arrange everything and all you have to do is show up and climb aboard. You will receive an email once the bus "tilts" so you know it's a go.
